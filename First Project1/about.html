About BCA Quiz

The BCA Quiz is an interactive online platform designed for BCA students to test and enhance their knowledge in various subjects like Data Structures, Operating Systems, Database Management, Networking, and Programming Languages.

This project aims to make learning fun and engaging through multiple-choice questions, instant feedback, and score tracking. Students can practice anytime and prepare for exams effectively.

Features:

  • Subject-wise quizzes
  • Instant result evaluation
  • User-friendly interface
  • Progress tracking

Developed as a BCA 4th Semester Project by students for students.
